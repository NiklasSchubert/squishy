Choose which transcoding presets you want to use with Squishy. These presets will determine the quality and format of your transcoded (squished) videos.

These presets focus on excellent video quality while achieving good compression. They use the HEVC (H.265) codec with CRF-based quality settings, resulting in efficient file sizes with minimal quality loss.

Features:

  • HEVC (H.265) video codec
  • Quality-based CRF encoding
  • MKV container format
  • Excellent quality/size ratio
  • Hardware acceleration support
  • May not be compatible with all devices

These presets focus on maximum compatibility across devices. They use the H.264 codec with MP4 container, ensuring your videos will play on almost any device, including older TVs, phones, and tablets.

Features:

  • H.264 video codec
  • Bitrate-based encoding
  • MP4 container format
  • Wider device compatibility
  • Hardware acceleration support
  • Larger file sizes than HEVC

These are the default presets that come with Squishy. They offer a balanced approach with HEVC encoding and quality-based settings.

Features:

  • HEVC (H.265) video codec
  • Quality-based CRF encoding
  • MKV container format
  • Three quality levels: high, medium, low
  • Hardware acceleration support

Note: You can always customize and add more presets later from the Admin Dashboard.
